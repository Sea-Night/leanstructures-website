LEAN structures

A structural engineering consultancy finding clean, efficient and sustainable design solutions.

Structure that does exactly enough, and no more.

LEAN structures is a structural design practice based in Monmouthshire, working across London, Bristol, and the Welsh borders. We focus on finding clean design solutions that are efficient, economical and sustainable, in masonry, steel, timber, bamboo, glass, and concrete.

Our philosophy

LEAN design

50%

The average utilisation of structure in modern buildings — the rest is often over-specified, adding cost and embodied carbon without adding performance.

This is partly a result of designers protecting themselves from late design changes, and partly a fractured industry that undervalues collaboration and communication. LEAN design requires greater input at early project stages and more effective communication between disciplines — the result is high-performance, economical, low-waste structures built to meet the demands of an ever-changing building environment.

What we do

Innovative engineering

Building control submissions

Calculations, reports and structural drawings prepared to support building control approval, from concept through to construction-ready detail.

Sustainable engineering

PassivHaus structural design, natural and green materials, and embodied carbon assessment for clients building with sustainability as a core requirement, not an afterthought.

Heritage buildings

Historic building analysis, remediation strategies, seismic strengthening, and sympathetic renovation or modification of structures with existing character worth preserving.

Feature structures

Pop-up structures, feature staircases, bespoke furniture, art installations, and other one-off pieces that need engineering rigour without losing their design intent.
